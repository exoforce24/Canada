📚 Canada Tips & Safety

🐻 Bear Safety (Rockies)

  • Buy bear spray in Banff/Canmore (can't fly with it)
  • Make noise on trails — clap, talk, sing
  • Never approach or feed wildlife
  • Store food in car trunk, never in cabin
  • If you see a bear: stay calm, back away slowly, don't run
  • Black bear: make yourself big, be loud
  • Grizzly: play dead if it charges, protect neck

💰 Tipping Guide

  • Restaurants: 15-20% (expected, not optional)
  • Bars: $1-2 per drink or 15%
  • Taxis / Uber: 15%
  • Hotels: $2-5 per night housekeeping
  • Tour guides: 15-20% of tour cost
  • Valet: $2-5
  • Tip is before tax in Canada

🚘 Driving Tips

  • Speed limits: 100-110 km/h highways, 50 km/h towns
  • Icefields Parkway: 90 km/h, watch for wildlife
  • Fuel up before Icefields Parkway (limited stations)
  • Right turn on red is allowed (unless signed)
  • Headlights on at all times (law in Canada)
  • Watch for wildlife at dawn/dusk on mountain roads
  • Park regulations: must stop for wildlife on road

🇨🇦 General Tips

  • Currency: CAD ($1 SGD ≈ $0.97 CAD)
  • Tap-to-pay works almost everywhere
  • Water is safe to drink from taps
  • Parks pass: Buy a Discovery Pass ($145 CAD) for all national parks
  • Tax: Not included in prices (5-15% depending on province)
  • Download offline maps (no signal in mountain areas)
  • Carry layers — mountain weather changes fast

🅿️ Parking Tips

Moraine Lake ⚠️

Day 10

NO public parking. Must take Parks Canada shuttle from Lake Louise. You have 11:30 AM shuttle booked. Park at Lake Louise overflow lot.

Lake Louise

Day 8 & 10

Lot fills by 6 AM in summer. Your 3:45 AM wake-up should get you a spot. Overflow lot available. $21/day.

Johnston Canyon

Day 5

Arrive before 10 AM. Main lot fills fast. Overflow lot 1 km east on Bow Valley Parkway. Free.

Lynn Canyon

Day 2

Free parking lot at Lynn Canyon Park. No car needed — you're using public transit (Bus 228)

Emerald Lake

Day 7

Small lot fills by 10 AM on weekends. Your 9:30 AM canoe should be fine on a weekday. Free.

Peyto Lake

Day 10

New parking lot with $21 day pass (Parks pass may cover it). Short walk to viewpoint.

Maligne Lake

Day 12

Large free lot. Fills for Spirit Island cruises. Arrive 15 min before your cruise time.

Takakkaw Falls

Day 7

Road has tight switchbacks — no trailers. BMW 3 Series will be fine. Free lot at the falls.

Old Montreal / Quebec

Days 17-20

Street parking is tricky. Use Indigo lots ($15-25/day). Hotel parking if available. Download P$ app.
