📚 Canada Tips & Safety

🐻 Bear Safety (Rockies)

  • Buy bear spray in Banff/Canmore (can't fly with it)
  • Make noise on trails — clap, talk, sing
  • Never approach or feed wildlife
  • Store food in car trunk, never in cabin
  • If you see a bear: stay calm, back away slowly, don't run
  • Black bear: make yourself big, be loud
  • Grizzly: play dead if it charges, protect neck

💰 Tipping Guide

  • Restaurants: 15-20% (expected, not optional)
  • Bars: $1-2 per drink or 15%
  • Taxis / Uber: 15%
  • Hotels: $2-5 per night housekeeping
  • Tour guides: 15-20% of tour cost
  • Valet: $2-5
  • Tip is before tax in Canada

🚘 Driving Tips

  • Speed limits: 100-110 km/h highways, 50 km/h towns
  • Icefields Parkway: 90 km/h, watch for wildlife
  • Fuel up before Icefields Parkway (limited stations)
  • Right turn on red is allowed (unless signed)
  • Headlights on at all times (law in Canada)
  • Watch for wildlife at dawn/dusk on mountain roads
  • Park regulations: must stop for wildlife on road

🇨🇦 General Tips

  • Currency: CAD ($1 SGD ≈ $0.97 CAD)
  • Tap-to-pay works almost everywhere
  • Water is safe to drink from taps
  • Parks pass: Buy a Discovery Pass ($145 CAD) for all national parks
  • Tax: Not included in prices (5-15% depending on province)
  • Download offline maps (no signal in mountain areas)
  • Carry layers — mountain weather changes fast
